    Fun Facts all about dogs!
    Unique nose prints: Each dog's nose print is one-of-a-kind, just like a human's fingerprints. Superior sense of smell: A dog's sense of smell is legendary, possibly up to 100,000 times stronger than a human's. Emotional detection: Dogs can smell emotional and physical changes in humans, like the hormones released when we are scared or happy. They can even be trained to detect diseases like cancer and diabetes. Three eyelids: Dogs have a third eyelid that helps protect their eyes and keep them moist.

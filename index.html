    Fun Facts all about dogs!

    Unique nose prints: Each dog's nose print is one-of-a-kind, just like a human's fingerprints.
    Sense of smell: A dog's sense of smell is legendary, possibly up to 100,000 times stronger than a human's.
    Emotional detection: Dogs can smell emotional and physical changes in humans.
    Three eyelids: Dogs have a third eyelid that helps protect their eyes and keep them moist.
